Step by Step

UK Student Visa Process

1
Choose UKVI Licensed Sponsor
Only universities/colleges on the UKVI register of licensed sponsors can issue a CAS. Verify your institution before applying.
2
Take IELTS UKVI or SELT Test
Must be an approved Secure English Language Test (SELT). Regular IELTS is accepted for most universities. Minimum 6.0 usually required.
3
Apply & Receive CAS
Apply to university, receive offer letter, then accept and receive your Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) number.
4
Apply Online for Student Visa
Apply on gov.uk website. You can apply from Nepal up to 6 months before course start. Pay £363 visa fee + IHS surcharge.
5
Visit VFS Centre in Kathmandu
Book appointment at VFS Global Kathmandu for biometrics and document submission. Usually within 5-7 days.
6
Receive BRP & Travel
Once approved, collect your Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) within 10 days of arrival at your UK address.
