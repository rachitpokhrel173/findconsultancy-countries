Step by Step

Canada Study Permit Process

1
Choose DLI Institution
Must apply to a Designated Learning Institution (DLI). Check the IRCC DLI list before applying anywhere.
2
Get IELTS Score
Minimum 6.0 overall with no band below 6.0 for SDS stream. Higher scores improve chances.
3
Get Letter of Acceptance
Apply to universities/colleges and receive your official Letter of Acceptance (LOA) — required for study permit.
4
Open GIC Account (SDS)
For SDS fast-track, deposit CAD 10,200 in a Guaranteed Investment Certificate with a Canadian bank partner before applying.
5
Apply for Study Permit Online
Apply via IRCC portal. Upload LOA, GIC, IELTS, medical, financial documents. Pay CAD 150 + biometrics CAD 85.
6
Biometrics at VFS Centre
Book biometrics appointment at VFS Global Kathmandu. Takes 10-15 minutes.
7
Port of Entry Letter & Fly!
Receive Port of Entry Letter of Introduction. Get study permit stamped at Canadian border upon arrival.
