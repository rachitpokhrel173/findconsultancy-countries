Step by Step

F-1 Visa Process from Nepal

1
Take TOEFL / IELTS & GRE/GMAT
TOEFL iBT 80+ or IELTS 6.5+ required. GRE needed for most graduate programs. Plan 6-12 months ahead.
2
Apply to Universities (Common App / Direct)
Apply to 5-8 universities. Undergrad uses Common App. Grad programs apply directly. Strong SOP is critical.
3
Receive I-20 Form
Once admitted, university issues I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility). This is your most important document for visa.
4
Pay SEVIS Fee (USD 350)
Pay the Student and Exchange Visitor Information System fee at FMJfee.com before scheduling your visa interview.
5
Fill DS-160 & Schedule Interview
Complete DS-160 online form. Schedule F-1 visa interview at US Embassy Maharajgunj, Kathmandu. Pay USD 185.
6
Attend Visa Interview
The interview is the hardest part. Be confident. Show strong ties to Nepal. Explain clearly why you chose the specific university and program.
7
Visa Approved — Fly to USA!
Enter USA no earlier than 30 days before your program start date on the I-20.
