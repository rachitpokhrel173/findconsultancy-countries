🏆 GKS — Korean Government Scholarship

The Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) is one of the best opportunities for Nepali students. It covers full tuition, monthly stipend (KRW 900,000–1,000,000), airfare, health insurance, and Korean language training. Highly competitive — apply through NIIED in January-February each year.

Step by Step

South Korea Student Visa Process

1
Learn Korean or Find English Program
Start TOPIK preparation or enroll in Korean language classes in Nepal. Many universities offer English programs — check for IELTS/TOEFL requirements.
2
Apply for GKS Scholarship (Optional but Recommended)
Apply every January-February through NIIED website. If selected, everything is covered. Requires good academic record and motivation letter.
3
Apply to University/Language School
Apply directly or through a consultancy. Most Korean universities have international admissions offices. Get your admission letter.
4
Apply for D-2 or D-4 Visa
Go to Korean Embassy Kathmandu (Maharajgunj). Submit admission letter, financial proof, medical certificate, and application form.
5
Get Alien Registration Card (ARC)
Within 90 days of arriving in Korea, register at local immigration office to get your Alien Registration Card. Required for everything.
6
Start Studies & Find Part-Time Work
Convenience stores, restaurants, and tutoring are common part-time jobs. Pay is KRW 9,860/hr (minimum wage).
